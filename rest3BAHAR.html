About


Cafe Bahar started in 1973 by Mr. Syed Hussain Bolooki as a small tea stall and a provisional store. As the years went by, they began serving Hyderabadi cuisine especially Biryani and expanding the area in the form of a small common restaurant, which over time added an A/C Family Restaurant with few more cuisines to our menu. Our speciality and identity is famous across the borders with the authentic taste of Hyderabadi Biryani, Irani Haleem, Irani Chai, Osmania Biscuits and lots more.
